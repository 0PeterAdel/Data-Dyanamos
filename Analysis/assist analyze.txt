#### 1. **Demographic Insights**

##### a. Gender and Ethnic Composition
```python
# Gender and Ethnic composition
composition = df.groupby(['Gender', 'Ethnicity']).size().reset_index(name='Counts')
print(composition)
sns.countplot(x='Gender', hue='Ethnicity', data=df)
plt.title('Gender and Ethnic Composition of Workforce')
plt.show()
```

##### b. Age Distribution Across Departments
```python
# Age distribution across departments
sns.boxplot(x='Department', y='Age', data=df)
plt.title('Age Distribution Across Departments')
plt.xticks(rotation=45)
plt.show()
```

##### c. Key Factors Driving Employee Attrition
```python
# Correlation heatmap for attrition
correlation_matrix = df.corr()
sns.heatmap(correlation_matrix, annot=True, fmt=".2f")
plt.title('Correlation Matrix for Employee Attrition Factors')
plt.show()
```

##### d. Attrition Across Demographics and Departments
```python
# Attrition across demographics and departments
sns.countplot(x='Department', hue='Attrition', data=df)
plt.title('Attrition Across Departments')
plt.show()

# Demographic-wise attrition
sns.countplot(x='Gender', hue='Attrition', data=df)
plt.title('Attrition Across Genders')
plt.show()
```

##### e. Correlation Between DistanceFromHome and Attrition
```python
sns.scatterplot(x='DistanceFromHome', y='Attrition', data=df, hue='Attrition')

plt.show()
```

##### f. Salary Differences Across Groups
```python
sns.boxplot(x='Department', y='Salary', hue='Gender', data=df)

```
